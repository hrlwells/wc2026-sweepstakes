# Beach-Muscles Workout Bank — 16 Weeks

One gym session per week, hitting chest, back, shoulders and arms. Progressive overload within each block, with fresh exercise variations between blocks to keep the stimulus fresh. Deload/recovery weeks are deliberately easy — that's where the growth locks in.


## Block · Foundation

### Week 1 — Foundation — groove & baseline
*Chest · Back · Shoulders · Arms*

Week 1. Pick weights you could get ~2 more reps with (RPE 7). Write down every top-set weight — this is the baseline you'll beat all block.

**Warm-up:** 5 min easy cardio, then 2 light ramp-up sets on the first press and first row.

| Exercise | Sets | Reps | RPE | Rest | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Barbell Bench Press | 3 | 10–12 | 7 | 2–3 min | Primary chest. Log your top-set weight. |
| Barbell Row (overhand) | 3 | 10–12 | 7 | 2–3 min | Back thickness. Controlled, no heaving. |
| Incline Dumbbell Press | 3 | 10–12 | 7 | 90 sec | Upper chest. |
| Lat Pulldown | 3 | 10–12 | 7 | 90 sec | Back width. Full stretch at the top. |
| Cable Fly (mid) | 3 | 10–12 | 7 | 60 sec | Chest isolation + stretch. |
| Face Pull | 3 | 10–12 | 7 | 60 sec | Rear delts / posture. |
| Dumbbell Curl | 3 | 10–12 | 7 | 60 sec | Biceps. |
| Cable Triceps Pushdown | 3 | 10–12 | 7 | 60 sec | Triceps. |
| Dumbbell Lateral Raise | 3 | 10–12 | 7 | 60 sec | Beach bonus — delt width. |

### Week 11 — Bucks week — keep it simple  _( BUCKS )_
*Full-body / whatever's available*

BUCKS WEEK (5–11 Oct) — you're away. One optional session, bodyweight or hotel gym: push-ups, dips, DB curls, lateral raises if there are dumbbells. 3 rounds, stop 2 reps short. Goal is to maintain, not to grow. Enjoy the trip; don't stress if it's skipped.

**Warm-up:** Whatever you've got — a few minutes of movement first.

| Exercise | Sets | Reps | RPE | Rest | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Push-ups | 3 | 12–15 / 30–45s | 6 | 60 sec | Chest/triceps — elevate feet if too easy. |
| Dips or Bench Dips | 3 | 12–15 / 30–45s | 6 | 60 sec | Triceps/chest. |
| Dumbbell/Backpack Curls | 3 | 12–15 / 30–45s | 6 | 60 sec | Biceps — improvise load. |
| Lateral Raises (if DBs available) | 3 | 12–15 / 30–45s | 6 | 60 sec | Delts. |
| Plank | 3 | 12–15 / 30–45s | 6 | 60 sec | Core finisher. |
